Products

Summer Intern

The Summer Intern program is an unpaid internship program for students in their second and third year of university studies, where they work with startup companies for a period of 6 - 12 weeks, to garner more experience.

Graduate Talent

The Graduate Talent program is a 6 weeks fellowship program for students in their final year of their university studies. They are enrolled in a training program in a bootcamp style, after which they are placed into organizations for a graduate internship

Expert Talent

The Expert Talent program is a job placement for candidate who are actively searching for a job to advance their career. Candidates may include people already in the workfoce who have experience and expertise in their field
